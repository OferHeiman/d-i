The Avengers

The Avengers were a team of extraordinary individuals,
with either superpowers or other special characteristics.
Its purpose was to protect global stability from inner or extraterrestrial threats.
Though primarily affiliated with the United States of America,
they worked with the peaceful interests of the whole world in mind
rather than a specific country or organization.
The Avengers were first assembled by S.H.I.E.L.D. when Loki
invaded Earth with his Chitauri army to conquer the planet.
The team, consisting of Iron Man, Thor, Captain America,
Black Widow, Hawkeye and Hulk, defeated Loki.
